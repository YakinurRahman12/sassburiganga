About Buriganga

Developer's Information

Buriganga is fish recognition app developed by Yakinur Rahman as result of Mobile Application course's lab. It recognizes five fish species among which four are native species. Users can provide their feedbacks through comments and ratings. This app ensures proper usability of users.

Key Features

  • Fish recognition
  • Easy usability
  • Provides location awareness
  • Users can provide feedbacks
  • Secure authentication
